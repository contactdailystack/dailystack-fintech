# PicksWise — Reports Wireframe (Screen 5)

Purpose: Visualize spending by category, monthly trends, and comparisons with simple charts and natural-language insights.

Key sections: Overview (donut + totals) → Trend (monthly sparkline) → Top categories list → Compare view toggle → Export/share CTA

Mobile layout: Overview card top, trend chart mid, list of categories with progress bars, bottom CTA to export or save snapshot.

Interactions: Tap category to drill into transactions; toggle compare to previous month; date range picker.

States: Empty (show sample charts), Loading (skeleton cards), Error (retry & offline fallback).

Accessibility: Simplified charts with accessible labels, aria-description for charts, contrast checks.
